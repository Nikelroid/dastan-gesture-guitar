Melody Hand · lead instrument

Raise or lower your other hand to pick the note — it snaps to the scale, one clean note per lane, gliding smoothly between them. The note sounds only while your index finger is up: lower it and the sound stops instantly, flick it to articulate notes (its fingertip glows gold while sounding). Want every microtone and your own hand vibrato instead? Switch Pitch mode to Continuous and a semitone ruler with a live tuner readout (A4 +12¢) appears. The melody hand is drawn in blue and never triggers chords.
